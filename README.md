# 🌀 Emote Bro: The AI That Sees You
**Emote Bro** is a high-EQ (Emotional Intelligence) digital mentor that doesn't just read what you say—it reads how you look when you say it. By fusing real-time facial expression tracking with deep-learning sentiment analysis, it bridges the gap between digital interaction and human empathy.

---

## ⚡ Our Main USP: The "Masking" Detector
Most AI models are **emotionally blind**. If you type *"I'm fine"* while looking devastated, a standard chatbot believes you. 

**Emote Bro’s "Affective Logic"** detects the disconnect. It compares your facial geometry to your words to identify **Emotional Masking**. If your face is smiling but your tone is heavy, the AI shifts its persona from a casual assistant to a supportive mentor, acknowledging the bravery it takes to "mask" your feelings.



---

## 🛠️ The Tech Stack (The Multimodal Handshake)
We’ve blended edge-computing with state-of-the-art AI to create a seamless, private, and instant experience:

* **The Vision (Edge AI):** Uses **MediaPipe** to track 478 3D facial landmarks directly in your browser. This ensures your video never leaves your device—only the "blendshape" scores (like smile intensity) are processed.
* **The Ears (Neural Audio):** Leverages **Whisper-v3** via **Groq** for lightning-fast voice-to-text transcription.
* **The Heart (Sentiment NLP):** A **DistilBERT** Transformer model that classifies text into 6 core human emotions.
* **The Brain (LLM Orchestrator):** **Llama-3.1-8B** synthesizes your face, your words, and your history to generate a context-aware response.
* **The Memory (Persistent Vibe):** A **MySQL** backend that tracks your "Vibe History," allowing the AI to notice patterns and help you break out of negative spirals.
